Survive as long as you can!

Boomboy is an endless runner where you can jump, slide, and shoot to dodge and take care of obstacles in your path.

You'll encounter poison frogs, faceless monkeys, blood ravens, and zombie wolves!

Your score will increase as long as your don't lose all of your health.

 

Rack up those points and show your friends how far you can go in this endless runner.
